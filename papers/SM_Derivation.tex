\begin{abstract}
We record a gated map from excitations of the gauged Hopf lattice
(product Yang--Mills + Hopfions + holonomy/braiding) onto the Standard Model
field content under $G=\mathrm{SU}(3)_c\times\mathrm{SU}(2)_L\times\mathrm{U}(1)_Y$.
Phase~2.1 (Gate~SM-1) requires correct representations and electric charges
$Q=T_3+Y/2$. Three generation \emph{copies} are listed via braiding-layer index;
Yukawa hierarchies and RG flow are deferred to Gates~SM-2/SM-3.
Core locks $W_g=350/\pi$, $\kappa\approx 0.85$, $\phi_b^\star\approx 0.8145$ remain
frozen. No gravity claim is made.
\end{abstract}
